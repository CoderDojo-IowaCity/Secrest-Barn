Directions

 Here are the directions to the Secrest 1883 Octagonal Barn

FROM INTERSTATE 80

go to the West Branch exit (#254);
go south 4 miles on X30 to Downey
Turn right (west) on Cedar Road (gravel),
At the end of the street turn left (south) on Adams Road,
Follow the gravel road about 1 mile, Adams curves to the right (west)
barn is on right (north) side

FROM IOWA CITY ON HIGHWAY 6

Go east on highway 6,
go about 5 miles to the bend in the road and turn left (north) on Oasis (gravel) Road
Go 1 mile and turn right (east) on Osage.
Barn is 1 mile on left (north).
